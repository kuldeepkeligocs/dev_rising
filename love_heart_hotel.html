About the Hotel

  • Brand New B&B with 3 star quality rooms and facilities.
  • Small Garden along with an attached Apple Orchard.
  • Luxurious rooms with Mountain View AND Balcony.

Welcome to Hotel Love Heart, sheltered in the serene beauty of Manali, Himachal Pradesh. Located on Club House Manu Temple Road, just 900 m from Hadimba Devi Temple and 1.5 Kms from Mall Road, Old Manali, our hotel offers a tranquil retreat amidst the breathtaking landscapes of the Himalayas. Whether you're seeking adventure in the great outdoors or a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, our prime location provides the perfect base for exploring all that this enchanting region has to offer. At Hotel Love Heart, we pride ourselves on offering more than just accommodation – we offer an experience. Our cozy rooms and suites are designed to provide the utmost comfort, with modern amenities and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Indulge in the flavors of Himachali cuisine at our onsite restaurant, where every dish is crafted with care using locally sourced ingredients. After a day of exploring, unwind with a soothing massage at our spa, or simply relax by the fireplace with a book from our library. For those seeking adventure, our knowledgeable staff can assist you in planning a variety of outdoor activities, from trekking and mountain biking to river rafting and paragliding. And with the vibrant markets and cultural attractions of Old Manali just a stone's throw away, there's always something new to discover. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway, a family vacation, or a romantic escape, Hotel Love Heart is your home away from home in the heart of the Himalayas. We look forward to welcoming you to our slice of paradise and helping you create memories that will last a lifetime.
